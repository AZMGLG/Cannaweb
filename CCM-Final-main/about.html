Our Philosophy

Cannabis Madrid was established with a singular vision: to create a refined, safe, and private environment for cannabis enthusiasts in the capital. We operate as a private non-profit association, strictly adhering to Spanish regulations while setting a new standard for member experience.

Privacy

Community

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

Membership is by invitation or endorsement. You can request an interview via our WhatsApp contact to begin the registration process.

Is it legal?

Yes, we operate as a private cannabis social club (CSC) under Spanish law, serving only registered members in a private space.

Do you sell to tourists?

We do not "sell" to the public. We are a members-only association. Tourists may join if they meet the legal requirements and complete the registration process.
